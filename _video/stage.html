ToneWise

In Mandarin the tone is the word: 水饺 (shuǐjiǎo, "dumplings") vs 睡觉 (shuìjiào, "sleep"). Almost every app drills flashcards but never actually hears your tones — and never lets you hear a native speaker say it back. ToneWise does both.

What it does
1
Pick a real scenario (ordering food, travel, a meeting) and your HSK level.
2
Speak your reply — ToneWise measures the actual pitch of your voice.
3
Every syllable's tone is scored — green = right, red = wrong — next to your pitch curve.
4
Then Lin Wei says it back in a natural native voice, and your misses go to a spaced-repetition queue.
