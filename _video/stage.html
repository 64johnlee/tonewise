ToneWise

Say the right syllable with the wrong tone and 水饺 (shuǐjiǎo, "dumplings") becomes 睡觉 (shuìjiào, "sleep"). Almost every app drills flashcards and never corrects your tones in a real conversation. ToneWise fixes exactly that.

What it does
1
Pick a real scenario (ordering food, travel, a work meeting) and your HSK level.
2
Have an actual back-and-forth with Lin Wei, an AI native speaker.
3
After every reply: a tone & grammar score, the specific tone errors it heard, pinyin + a more natural phrasing.
4
A spaced-repetition queue built from your actual mistakes — so you drill what you personally get wrong.
